How to use the Salary Calculator

This tool helps freelancers, contractors, and hourly employees understand their gross income. By inputting your hourly rate and working schedule, we calculate your total earnings.

The Formula

  • Weekly: Wage × Hours × Days
  • Yearly: Weekly Pay × 52 Weeks
  • Monthly: Yearly Pay ÷ 12 Months
