Understanding Loan EMI

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay to the bank every month to repay your loan.

How is it calculated?

We use the standard reducing balance method used by most banks. The formula accounts for the principal amount, the annual interest rate, and the loan tenure in months.
